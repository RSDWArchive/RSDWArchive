==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
Anyone who worked with Alric knew about the Subjugation Staff. It would thud, thud, thud on the cobblestone floor as he approached. It was a welcome warning that Alric was arriving, but it came with notes of dread. 

The power of the Subjugation Staff came from what was rolled up and inserted into its middle. It was a demon pact, but one that had been defaulted on. A demon, not a human, had failed to keep their side of the bargain, and that gave the contract a radiating power. Alric captured that power within the staff. While he held it, it was as if all of demonkind owed him something.
