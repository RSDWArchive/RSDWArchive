Animal bones are surprisingly durable, especially those found on Ashenfall. The magic of the continent seeps into their marrow, slowly strengthening them and making them tougher. This goes some way to explaining the ferocity of ordinarily docile creatures, but it also helps when crafting tools. 

Cows and similar beasts were celebrated in the age of Guthix. The people of Brynmoor preferred to take an animal’s life only on the hunt or after long years of service. When an animal passed, it was thanked for its sacrifice, and the materials helped strengthen the village.
