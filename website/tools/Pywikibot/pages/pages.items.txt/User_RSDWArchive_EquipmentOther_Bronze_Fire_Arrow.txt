Deadly arrows made deadlier with fire. Each arrow burns with the fury reserved for the most unpleasant parts of Infernus. They sear, they singe, and they melt the flesh, turning their victim into little more than ashes floating away on the wind.

In the days of the great war against the Garou, the priests of Saradomin encouraged the use of these weapons. They saw it not just as a means of eradicating the hated beastmen, but to purify the world from their taint. Lougrim was a false goddess, in their eyes, and her followers had to be cleansed from existence.

Modern scholars would doubtless be confused at their monotheistic mindset, considering that the literal god of war, Bandos, walked beside them into battle. The mental gymnastics employed by the Brynmoor priesthood, could have rivalled any athlete in the Gielinor Games.
