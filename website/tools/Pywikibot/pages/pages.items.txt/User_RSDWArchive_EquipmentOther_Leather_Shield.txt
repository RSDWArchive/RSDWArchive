==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
A leather shield consists of a thick piece of leather stretched across a wooden frame. Wooden shields are bulky and heavy, making them more unwieldy. In contrast, a leather shield’s relative lightness makes it much easier to move into position. 

Leather shields were popular with the villagers of Bramblemead since the materials were comparatively easy to gather. Young warriors often wielded them as they accompanied druids into the woods, where the druids gathered the herbs for their medicines and rituals. 

The Children of Shadows also enjoyed leather shields, which they decorated with Zamorakian symbols, hoping to intimidate anyone who saw them.
