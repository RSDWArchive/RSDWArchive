Popular among gnomes, this sticky ball of fruits and vegetables is a combination of savoury and sweet that almost feels like it shouldn’t work. Yet it does.

Gnomish ambassadors would create large numbers of these sweet treats, piled high on silver platters, to delight visiting dignitaries at lavish receptions.
