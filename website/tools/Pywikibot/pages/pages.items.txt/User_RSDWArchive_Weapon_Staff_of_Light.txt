==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
In the third age, before someone could wield a staff of light, it would first need to be blessed by three people. 

A priest would bless it first, instilling the weapon with Saradomin’s wisdom. A monk would bless it second, imbuing the staff with the humility of Saradomin. Finally, an icyene general would give their blessing, dedicating the weapon to Saradomin’s righteousness. 

Those battlemages chosen to wield such a sacred weapon would dive into battle against Zamorak’s forces and believe themselves invulnerable. 

They were often proved wrong. 

Yet, those who carried the staff into battle seemed to survive longer than their compatriots. The defensive magic of the staff kept them alive longer than others. A demon once stole a staff and tried to use that power for himself. Still, when he tried to channel demonic power through the staff, it is said that the explosion could be seen from across the battlefield.
