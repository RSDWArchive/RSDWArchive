==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
It was late in the development of Fellhollow that the people needed armour. They made do with old hand me down leather and bronze gear, brought with them from Bramblemead, for the most part, because what need was there for armour when you were protected by dragons.

Kuldra would put a stop to that mindset in the end, but it was the Children of Shadows who forged the first steel gear in Fellhollow. One member, in particular, Sadia Vulketra, began to feel that the order lacked the strength it may one day need to defend against the Saradominist forces.

There was no war. No conflict like that in Emberwood, indeed the four major faiths got along rather well with one another. Yet, the history books told Sadia exactly what the priests of Saradomin would do to the worshippers of the god of chaos.

She forged a helm first, with the assistance of one of her many lovers, a blacksmith Amalgamated called Vesk. The first helmet was styled with great curling horns, stained black by the forge’s flames.

Naturally, this style didn’t prove popular with the rest of the village, as they began crafting steel helms of their own, favouring a more simplistic approach, and a feathered plume, to look fancy.
