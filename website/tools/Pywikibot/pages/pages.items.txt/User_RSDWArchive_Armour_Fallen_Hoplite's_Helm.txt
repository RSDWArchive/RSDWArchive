==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
Those who joining the Fellhollow military, who were not from the more noble bloodlines, would join the force known as the hoplites. This force of warrior farmers named themselves after the hoplite forces of Hallow, legendary forces spoken of in some of the Saradominist text.

They were disciplined in a way that earned them great respect. Each of them acting as the shield for the person next to them and a spear for the enemy in front. They wore armour reflecting some of the style of Hallow, with plateskirts rather than sturdy grieves, allowing for more freedom of movement, a trait they took great advantage of.
