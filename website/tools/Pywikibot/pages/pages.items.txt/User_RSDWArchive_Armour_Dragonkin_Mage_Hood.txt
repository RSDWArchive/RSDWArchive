==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
The dragonkin of Ashenfall all reveled in power, gaining elemental mastery from the strange anima of the land. Although their kind tended to ignore magic, in favour of alchemy and science, the dragonkin of Ashenfall enjoyed their time as sorcerer kings.

They were robes that empowered their magic, using alchemical techniques to create cloth that would resist their elemental powers. Fire would not burn so easily, cold would not damage the threads and lightning could be turned aside with a flick of a cloak. Though these techniques are lost to time, the echoes of their powerful resistance remains.

The hood was designed for two purposes. The shadow it cast upon the dragonkin features, made their visage scarier and intimidation easier. Goblins, humans and garou would cower from the reptilian grins that was all they could see in the darkness. The second was to hide their frustration at the foolishness of others, their fellow dragonkin included. Rolling eyes is much easier when no one else can see.
