==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
The name is a bit of a misnomer, since this amulet has very little to do with the river Salve. Instead its name refers to the crystal that traditionally sits at the heart. This crystal was first brought to the region by Saradominist priests, some time in the third age. They had harvested it from a cave near the river Salve and found ways to cultivate on Ashenfall’s soil, by utilising the natural magic infusing everything.

Sanctified by faith and magic, this amulet was deadly to the undead, and the dragonkin Lacrussa was fascinated with the properties. She stole the recipe for its creation and hid it within her vault, long before the withering curse spread over Fellhollow. Instead she had delved too deeply in her own experiments and discovered something buried within the earth that would twist and reanimate the dead.

This amulet allowed her to hold the forces back and keep them from overrunning her lab. That was, until, she met her end at Kuldra’s fangs.
