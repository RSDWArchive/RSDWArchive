==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
The dragonkin of Ashenfall all reveled in power, gaining elemental mastery from the strange anima of the land. Although their kind tended to ignore magic, in favour of alchemy and science, the dragonkin of Ashenfall enjoyed their time as sorcerer kings.

They were robes that empowered their magic, using alchemical techniques to create cloth that would resist their elemental powers. Fire would not burn so easily, cold would not damage the threads and lightning could be turned aside with a flick of a cloak. Though these techniques are lost to time, the echoes of their powerful resistance remains.

Lacrussa was the only dragonkin to resist the alure of magic. She insisted that science was the greater power, capable of almost anything with time and study. Lacrussa wore the robes as an a small act of rebellion, claiming that magic would always be overcome in the end.

Unlike her companions, Lacrussa refused to decorate her robes. She took no joy in precious stones, or shining metals. She refused to embroider designs across the cloak. She did, however, use alchemical techniques to enhance the defensive power of the robes. Something the other dragonkin soon came to copy, proving, in her mind, the superiority of science.
