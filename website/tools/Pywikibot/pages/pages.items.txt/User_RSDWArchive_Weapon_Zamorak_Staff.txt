==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
There are myths and anecdotes about Zamorak appearing to the inhabitants of Dowdun Reach. Few truly believe them. Zamorak is not one for hiding his presence: he's more theatrical than that.

It may be hard to believe, but some have tried to 'summon' Zamorak to Dowdun Reach. Call it the hubris of a demon summoner: a student feels they have mastered the art of summoning demons, so believes the next rung on the ladder is god. Never mind that Zamorak has no ounce of demon within him.

Zamorak can be accused of many things, but he does have a sense of humour. These attempts have been greeted with a gift. He has been known to kick a bloodveld through the portal, or another creature he has to hand. It gives the demon summoner a little spot-test.
