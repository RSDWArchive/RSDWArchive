==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
Hardened leather is a sturdier defence, able to turn away more powerful attacks. Favoured by hunters and rangers, who like the defence it offers without the reduction in mobility brought on by sturdier armour.

In poor weather, particularly rain and cold, the armour would create a distinctive creaking noise. Barely audible for the most part, but in the silence of night it felt like a deafening roar. Wild Knights made up for the sound of their armour, as they crept through the streets to rescue Amalgamated, by using simple spells to distract people.

They called animal spirits, notably crows, ravens and magpies, which would caw angrily at anyone nearby. This would earn the term “dark tidings”, referring to whenever the Wild Knights were seen near town – in reference to the magpie spirits they would summon.
