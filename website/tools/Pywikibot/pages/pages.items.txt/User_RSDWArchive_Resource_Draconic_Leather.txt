Tanned hide infused with a dragon’s vigor, draconic leather was a gamechanging discovery to the people of Fellhollow. It was as flexible as normal leather, but more resilient than even hardened leather, and it could be fashioned into a variety of shapes useful in a variety of armoured styles.
