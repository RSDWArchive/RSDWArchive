Contrary to popular belief, a goblin is as smart as any other species. It was Bandos that instilled within them the notion that intelligence was a sin, and demonstrating your education was something to be discouraged. As a result, goblins often go out of their way to make themselves seem less intelligent than they really are.

Hence “swingslash”, the name given to this axe because of how it swings through the air, slashing anything it hits. It’s so reductive and silly, it can only be an overcorrection, a desperate attempt by the creator to seem dumber than they are.
