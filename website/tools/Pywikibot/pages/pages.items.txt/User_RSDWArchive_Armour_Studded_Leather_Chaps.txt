==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
Reinforced leather, with metal plates woven throughout. The plates rest underneath leather coverings, with metal studs to hold them in place, giving the armour its name. Favoured by scouts and hunters for the mixture of agility and defence. 

While the design of studded leather made it excellent for hunters and invaluable in battles against the garou, it fell short against those who knew its construction. Betrayal is a constant foe, and once the garou war had come to a close, the Wild Knights found themselves at the mercy of it. 

Though there were more dangerous threats, with the dragons that appeared out of nowhere, there were always some that prioritized power and persecution over common sense. Daggers emerged from the shadows and drove between the plates sewn into the leather. The poison on the tips of these blades ended the life of more than a few Wild Knights. 

The assassins called themselves the “Glass Knives” in memory of the serial killer that took the lives of so many Amalgamated. To them, the strangeness of the knights was a greater threat than the dragons, and they were happy to pay the terrible price that their actions would cost.
