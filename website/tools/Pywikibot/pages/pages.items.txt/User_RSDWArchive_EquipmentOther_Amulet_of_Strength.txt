Might makes right, is the cry of every wannabe despot. Bandos in particular was very fond of this statement. This amulet upholds that notion and does its best to help make sure you are the most correct person around.

It imbues your muscles with body-aligned anima, the magic strengthening the sinew and fortifying your bones. It adds greater force to your swing that will crush your enemies before you.

The goblin general, Snotknucke, wore this amulet and attempted to hunt down Velgar. He believed that, with its magic, he could fell the beast in a single swing of his club.
