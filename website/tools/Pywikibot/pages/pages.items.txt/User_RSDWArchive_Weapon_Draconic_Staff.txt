==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
Rasthin was a powerful sorceress, even among the dragonkin. She had mastered all manner of spells, and was even schooled in the techniques from the previous Revision.

Still, magic as an art was largely looked down upon by dragonkin society. It was considered vulgar, and the obsession of children, and alchemy and science was the real way forward. In part it’s because of the connection between magic and the hated Elder Gods, and the dragonkin feared rousing, further, their anger.

But Rasthin knew the value of magic, as did the other members of her group. She created a staff, forged from glittering crystal and anima-infused iron, which allowed her to channel her magic into powerful spells.

Yet, even with a weapon as powerful as this, she discarded her magic when she uncovered the power of Ashenfall – preferring, instead, to enjoy her newfound connection with elemental flame.

Still, the remnants of the staff bristle with potential, and while they may never reach the might of their ancestor, they remain powerful magic tools just waiting to be crafted.
