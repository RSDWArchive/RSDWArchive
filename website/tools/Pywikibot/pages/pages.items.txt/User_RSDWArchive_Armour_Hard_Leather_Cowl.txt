==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
Hardened leather is a sturdier defence, able to turn away more powerful attacks. Favoured by hunters and rangers, who like the defence it offers without the reduction in mobility brought on by sturdier armour.

Wild Knight hunters would favour this armour when visiting the forests of Brynmoor. Hard leather was sturdy, and the colours blended more easily with the treeline, allowing them to stalk their prey more stealthily.

For those of Amalgamated heritage, the armour was more easily shaped around any inconvenient mutations, and many a ranger would theme their armour to match. Fire-touched amalgamated were poorly suited to the armour, cooking the leather as they went. But those affected by plant-like mutations would enjoy blending leather and leaf.
