==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
While metal may be the preferred choice of weapon makers, the granite maul stands as a testament to the raw power of stone. This legendary weapon, revered among hammer wielders, is nothing more than a massive, heavy stone affixed to a stick. Yet, in the hands of a skilled wielder, it becomes a force of unparalleled destruction. 

It was developed by ancient dwarves long ago after one dwarf got fed up with the rock hammer he was using to fight a nest of gargoyles. The beasts were strong and numerous, and their skin was almost impenetrable to conventional weaponry; only rock hammers seemed to hurt them. In frustration, one dwarf took the biggest hunk of granite he could find and turned it into a legend. 

Since that moment, the weapon has been refined, using various special components to make it more wieldy in combat. The original creator having done himself an injury with the recoil.
