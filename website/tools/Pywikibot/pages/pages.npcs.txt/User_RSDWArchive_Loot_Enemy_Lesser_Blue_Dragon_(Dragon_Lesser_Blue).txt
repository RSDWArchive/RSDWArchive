==Journal==
{{Journal_entry|
Lacrussa, the creator of the lesser blue dragons, saw life in a different way to you or I. While we might approach a new creature with wariness or curiousity, Lacrussa would approach it with an intellectual hunger. The creature would be mentally dissected for its physical suitability, it's cognitive capacity, or its magical resonance. She would turn it in her mind, this way and that, determining how it might factor into her experiments.

Then came dissections of a more physical nature. She would cut and dice and drain. She was an expert in biological reaction, forcing revulsion and attraction out of living matter. She would take the results of those combinations and cycle them through the same experiments. Cut and dice and drain.

Knowing that experimentation begat experimentation, it's hard to pinpoint the materials that gave us the lesser blue dragon. If there is one certainty, it is that Lacrussa herself is part of them.

There are indications that, around the time that the blue dragons moved to Dowdun, Lacrussa was experimenting with lightning. It is possible that Lacrussa found a means to bottle it in a lesser blue dragon.
