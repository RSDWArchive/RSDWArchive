==Journal==
{{Journal_entry|
The Abyssal Demon is a nightmare incarnated. Appearing suddenly out of nowhere and capable of tearing a man to shreds in a single swing.

No one knows how the demons arrived on Ashenfall, as they are not native to this plane. They dwell, as their name suggests, in the Abyss, the realm between worlds. They were cast out from their homeworld by the god Zamorak when he led the demon uprising on Infernus, and they have remained hateful ever since. Unable to strike at the god that banished them, they turn their fury on every living thing, existing for nothing more than to perpetuate suffering.

A necromancer and her Wild Knight ally were the first to encounter the beast. She had been investigating one of the dragonkin ruins, a vault buried deep into the soil, and when they broke the seal, the foul beast emerged.  At least, that’s what she claims. None have been able to venture deep enough into the vaults to confirm the truth of her words.

She and her companion vanished from the area soon after, their whereabouts unknown.
