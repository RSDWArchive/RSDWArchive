==Journal==
{{Journal_entry|
The terrorbird lives up to its name with both it’s violent tendencies and the nightmarish cries it makes in the shadows. There are few who can look into the eyes of a terrorbird and not regret every decision in their lives, leading to this moment.

Early garou tried to tame the creatures, but the terrorbird’s wild nature prevented them from accepting the yoke of domestication. Instead, they turned on their would-be masters, leading to an even the garou would forever refer to as “the night of green feathers”.

Best taken out from range, the terrorbird makes itself known by it’s distinctive war-warble, that strikes fear into the heart of any sane creature.
