Immune: Poison

When a fungal rat lives too long, it can consume so much pestilence and decay that it bloats beyond the size of its weaker kin. In this state it absorbs ambient anima, becoming stronger and deadlier, a monarch of its kind.

The plague king is bane to the healthy. It spreads sickness wherever it goes, becoming a terrible disease against the living.

It was the duty of the Wild Knights to slay any fungal rat in sight, to make sure that a plague king never had a chance to develop. With the passing of the Wild Knights of Bramblemead, the world finds itself in terrible danger, should this beast ever learn how to swim.
