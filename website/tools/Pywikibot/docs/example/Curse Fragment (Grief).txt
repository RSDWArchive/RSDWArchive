The '''Curse Fragment of Grief''' is a quest item used in the quest [[Withering Heights]]. It is used alongside the Curse Fragments of [[Curse Fragment (Despair)|Despair]] and [[Curse Fragment (Rage)|Rage]] to dispel the barrier to Imaru's lair within the [[Hope's Fall]] region of [[Fellhollow]].

It can be found within the [[Emberwood]] village within the region (of the same name) in Fellhollow.
