A '''Bone Pickaxe''' is a [[Power level|Tier 2]] [[Pickaxe]] that can be crafted at a [[Crafting Table]]. It can mine the same ores as the previous tiers, along with [[Copper Ore]], and [[Tin Ore]], dealing 15 damage to minable ores, and 30 damage with a Critical Swing.
