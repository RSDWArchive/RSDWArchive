'''Weapon Poison''' is a potion that can be created at level 15 Ranged. This allows the player to poison their weapon (excluding tools such as [[Pickaxes]] or [[Logging Axes]]), making their weapon or ammunition [[Poison|poisonous]] for two minutes.

==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
Used in crafting Poison Arrows or to coat your equipped Melee Weapon with Poison for a short time.

There are times in life where you really want someone to regret ever meeting you. It could be a long-held grudge, it could be on the battlefield, or it could be in a never-ending meeting that could so easily have been an email but has now persisted past your lunchbreak and you had so been looking forward to that sushi… ahem… sorry, where was I? Oh, yes, pain!

Poison is sometimes considered to be a coward’s weapon. Personally, I prefer to think of it as the smart weapon choice, after all, honour is only worth anything if you’re the winner of the battle. A corpse with honour still rots in the dirt. With a blade coated in poison, you can almost guarantee the death of your enemies, all it takes is a single nick and their fate is sealed.

An investment well worth making, if you ask me.
