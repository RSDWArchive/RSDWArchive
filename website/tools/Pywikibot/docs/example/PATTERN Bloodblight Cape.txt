'''PATTERN: Bloodblight Cape''' is a consumable item that unlocks creation of the [[Bloodblight Cape]].

==Location==
Go to the [[Bloodblight Castle]] in the South of the [[Bloodblight Swamp]]. In the center of the ruins of the castle will be the [[Bloodwood Tree|Bloodblight Tree]] with a large section of wall South of tree.  Use [[Windstep]] to a protruding ledge on the East side of the wall.  Use a [[Pickaxes|Pickaxe]] or [[Rocksplosion]] to break away the crumbling wall to reveal the chest containing the blueprint.
