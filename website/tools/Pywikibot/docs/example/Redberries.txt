'''Redberries''' are a Power Level 1 ingredient that can be collected from [[redberry bush]]es. When eaten, they provide 5 [[sustenance]]. Players can also find redberries in a [[Dragon Slayer Reward Pack]] after completing the [[Dragon Slayer#A Lost Adventurer...|Lost Adventurer]] part of the quest.
