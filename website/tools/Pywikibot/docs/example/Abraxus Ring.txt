The '''Abraxus Ring''' is a [[Trinkets|ring]] that is a quest reward for completing the [[What Remains is Written]] secondary [[quests|quest]]. Additional copies can be crafted from a [[Jeweler's Bench]]. While equipped, the ring has a chance to reduce the rune cost of utility spells when cast.
