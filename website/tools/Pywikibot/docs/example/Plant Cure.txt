'''Plant Cure''' is a potion that is unlocked at level 3 [[Farming]], and can be created at the [[stone range]] or the [[cooking range]]. Players can use the plant cure on only diseased plants to revive them to their previous healthy state. Using one plant cure grants 2 Farming experience.

==Journal==
{{Journal entry|
Fashioned from clean water, weeds and animal bones, this mixture leeches out the vitality from those ingredients and transfers it to the sick and dying plants in your care. Cooked over a range, this concoction was first thought to be the domain of druids and herblore masters, but in truth it was a cook who put the pieces together. 

Old mother{{sic}} Potterington was much beloved by the people of Bramblemead. On weekends, when people allowed themselves brief breaks from their toils, she would whip up a great batch of something delicious and simmering, and neighbouring farmers would drop by for a meal.

This kindness extended not only to people, but to her garden and farming patches too. She whipped up a broth so infused with nutrients that Brassica Prime himself is said to have blessed it. When fed to plants, their withering and wilting leaves would stiffen and return to vibrancy — life and vitality theirs once again.

Now you can brew such a concoction yourself, so that your plants might always life their healthiest days.
}}
